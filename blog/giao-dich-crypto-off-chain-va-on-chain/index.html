Giao Dch On-Chain L G?

Cc giao dch on-chain bitcoin c xc thc bi cc th o v c ghi li trn blockchain. Khi cc giao dch c thm vo s lnh, mng blockchain s c cp nht v phn phi.

Mt Hn Ch Ca Cc Giao Dch Bitcoin On-Chain

Giao Dch Off-Chain Hot ng Nh Th No?

Giao dch off-chain l bt k giao dch no c x l bn ngoi blockchain. Cc giao thc lp th hai ny nhm mc ch ph v cc sai st ca chui bng cch cho php giao dch r hn v nhanh hn.

Khng ging nh cc mng on-chain, c rt nhiu giao thc off-chain. Chng bao gm Mng Lightning, Mng Liquid v nhiu hn. Chng ta hy cng nghin cu su v n.

Mng Lightning

Mng Lightning l mt giao thc Lp 2 c xy dng trn blockchain ca Bitcoin cho php ngi dng tham gia vo cc giao dch khng gii hn ngay lp tc vi chi ph ti thiu. Mng Lightning cng cho php hon i nguyn t chui cho, mang li s tin li v linh hot hn m khng cn ngi gim st bn th ba.

Giao Dch Off-Chain C Tt Hn Giao Dch On-Chain Khng?

C giao dch off-chain v on-chain u mang li nhng li ch v hn ch c th cho ngi dng tin in t. Cng ngh blockchain c mt vn v kh nng m rng, c th c qun l thng qua cc gii php off-chain.
